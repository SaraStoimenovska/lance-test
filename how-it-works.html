How it works...

Follow our simple 5 step process
below and you will have your planning,
permission, consent order or architectual
plans in within 7 days*

Step 1

Get an instant quote for your planning permission, consent order architectual plans and your journey begins...

Once you have received our quote and you would like to go ahead, simply pay your £299.00 deposit and the process begins. If you have any questions before comiting to our service please email on info@arkifuture.com or call +44 (0) 000 0000 during office hours with your questions.


Step 2

Book your 3D property scan or send us your measurents and photographys...

Once the deposit has been made you will get a conformation email from us and a call from us to book our ‘techie’ person to pop around to your location to do a 3D scan of your house. Or if you did not require our 3D scanning service you can send in your measurements of your refurbishment/development along with photographs.


Step 3

Its time to meet our architectual designer...

We will arrange for video call with screen share at a convenient ime to talk about your development, refurbishment or new build project. We will discuss your needs and requirement and answer any call that you may have.

For example, you may have sketched out a new interior layout that wants refining to finalising we would advise your the best use of interor or exteria space. If you selected our ‘Feng Shui’ option in our instant quote we will suggest a layout that best reflects the Feng Shui principles.


Step 4

Our architectual team is busy designing and drawing up your plans...

After the design and property layout meeting our team of architectual designers gets to work. If you have ordered the standard package you will receive your draft plans with 14-21 days of step 3 above. Once you have your draft set of plans you get 3 sets (rounds) of unlimited changes to finalise your plans.

If you paid the additional premium of our 7 day service, You will receive them within 7 days of step 3 above.


Step 5

Bring your dream development to life with our
interior designer and 3D visualisation.

If you select this option for ‘interior design’ our designers will create 3D visuals of your project from the plans from step 4 above. We will know your styling requirements from our step 3 meeting above.This really helps in visualising and planning your development, prior to building. You can see some examples of our interior design samples.

For 3d walkthrough video this is a popular service for showing investors, clients the finished development.
